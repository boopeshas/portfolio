01 — ABOUT

Who I am

I'm a Mechanical Engineer working in a design-focused capacity at TCS, embedded with a large OEM mechanical & power-equipment manufacturing client. My day-to-day sits between the CAD model and the build schedule — coordinating design, engineering and manufacturing so that what's drawn matches what actually gets delivered.

Before that, I spent time on the shop floor — inside High Pressure Die Casting and Gravity Die Casting lines, and around precision machining and tolerance-critical tooling — which is where most of what I know about designing for manufacturability actually came from.

In 2023 I placed 1st nationally in the Dassault Systèmes Bracket Design‑a‑thon, optimising a mechanical bracket in SolidWorks against weight, stress and manufacturability constraints.
